Spring Events

Egg Festival— Spr 13:

The Egg Festival is an annual festival that takes place in the town. The main event of the Egg Festival is an egg hunt that the player can participate in. Whoever finds the most eggs around town wins a special prize. Exclusive items can also be bought at a shop during the festival.

Salmonberry Season— Spr 15-18:

Bushes all throughout the valley will produce salmonberries. If the player interacts with a bush ready to harvest, they can collect and sell the salmonberries for profit.

Flower Dance— Spr 24:

The Flower Dance is held in the far west of the Cindersap Forest. The player can take part in the flower dance by asking a single NPC to be their partner. The player will be rejected if they do not have a high enough friendship level with the NPC. Exclusive items can be purchased during the Flower Dance.
