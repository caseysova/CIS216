Fall Events

Blackberry Season— Fall 08-11:

Bushes all throughout the valley will produce blackberries. If the player interacts with a bush ready to harvest, they can collect and sell the blackberries for profit.

Stardew Valley Fair— Fall 16:

The Stardew Valley Fair is a festival that takes place in the town. The player can win Star Tokens by playing multiple games that can be found all throughout the town square. Star Tokens can also be earned by participating in the town's produce contest. Star Tokens can be traded in to earn exclusive prizes only available at the fair.

Spirit's Eve— Fall 27:

Spirit's Eve is a nighttime festival that occurs in the town. The main event of the festival is a haunted maze the player can particpate in. The player will even recieve a special prize for completing the maze. The player can also buy special items at the Spirit's Eve shop.
