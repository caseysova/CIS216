Winter Events

Festival of Ice— Win 08:

The Festival of Ice takes place in the Cindersap Forest. The main event of the festival is an ice fishing competition that the player can take part in. The festival also features ice sculptures, snowmen, igloos and a shop that sells seasonal items exclusive to the festival.

Night Market— Win 15-17:

The Night Market lasts for three nights and is held on the docks at the beach. The night market includes several shops, a submarine ride, deep-sea fishing and a mermaid show.

Feast of the Winter Star— Win 25:

The Feast of the Winter Star is an annual festival that takes place in the town. The main event of the festival is a secret gift-giving game. The player and NPCs are assigned random people to give gifts to. The presents are then opened during the Feast of the Winter Star.

Best Winter Crops

Due to poor weather conditions in game, there are no growable crops for winter.
