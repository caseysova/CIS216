Summer Events

Luau— Sum 11:

The Luau takes place at the beach. The player can take part in a communal potluck soup and add any one ingredient of their choosing. A special guest, the Governor of the valley, judges the quality of the soup. The player can also buy exclusive items at a shop during the Luau.

Extra Beach Forageables— Sum 12-14:

For three days the player can find additional beach forageables located at the beach.

Dance of the Moonlight Jellies— Sum 28:

The Dance of the Moonlight Jellies takes place on the docks at the beach. Everyone in town gathers on the docks to watch the migration of special glow jellyfish. The player can purchase special items during the Dance of the Moonlight Jellies.
