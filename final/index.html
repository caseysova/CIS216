About the Game

Stardew Valley is a situation role-playing video game in which players take on the role of a charcter who recently recieves a farm belonging to their deceased grandfather. In this open-ended game, players explore Stardew Valley while taking on tasks such as growing crops, raising livestock, crafting goods, mining for ore, fighting monsters, selling produce, and forming relationships with the townspeople.

Just like in real life, Stardew Valley has four seasons: Spring, Summer, Fall and Winter. Depending on the season the player is playing in, different activities and events will become avaialble for the player to discover. Each season in game also has crops, fish and forageables that are unique and exclusive to that season. Players will have to play through all the seasons in order to complete the game.

Stardew Valley was created by Eric Barone, or more commonly know as ConcernedApe. The game released in February 2016 for Microsoft Windows, but was later released for macOS, Linux, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, iOS, and Android. The game allows up to four players to play together online or local.
